Why Sponsor

We want you to be a part of the OwlHacks family, and we think that sponsoring OwlHacks has some clear advantages:

  • You have the opportunity to recruit, network with, and market your brand to some of the best student engineers on the east coast
  • Although students have complete autonomy over what they choose to develop, companies may influence them through OwlHacks facilitated marketing:
    • Mentoring
    • Tech Talks
    • Provide Project Ideas
    • Host API Demos
    • Give Out Prizes
  • You have a unique ability to serve the community in an efficient and public way by helping student engineers and designers learn how to build and utilize technology
  • Your company get a vantage-point on the bleeding edge of the technological landscape; it’s a great opportunity to hear tomorrow’s startup ideas today

How You Can Help

OwlHacks is a huge undertaking, and we need all the support that we can get. There are many ways to support us! Here are some ideas:

  • Donate financially to OwlHacks. This allows us the flexibility to spend money where we need it the most
  • Sponsor donations on behalf of hackers
  • Sponsor tools for students to use during the event

Help us make OwlHacks an incredible experience for learning and invention. Our sponsorship packages, listed on the next page, outline our monetary contribution model. The higher your contribution, the more access you get to the student developers at OwlHacks. We are always looking for new and exciting things for our event.

Please contact Owlhacks Team if you have any questions or wish to schedule a phone call with an OwlHacks organizer.
